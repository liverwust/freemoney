Welcome to the PSU Triangle scholarship application!

Please submit your responses no later than 11:59 PM on {{ deadline }}. Winners will be announced during the Corporation Meeting. Please note that, due to restrictions attached to our scholarship funds, we are not able to award any scholarships to graduating seniors at this time (i.e., undergraduates who will no longer be attending Penn State next semester).

To apply for the Triangle Academic Excellence Award and/or the Studious Pledge Award, you will need:

  • A copy of your advising transcript (see LionPATH for instructions)
  • A few minutes to provide basic contact and demographic information about yourself

To apply for other awards, you may want to check the requirements for each award, but you will generally need:

  • Enough time to answer one or more essay questions related to your accomplishments, experiences, and/or goals (only for certain awards)
  • To have met with a faculty member -- please contact me if I didn't already get in touch with you (only for Giff Albright award)
  • Additional demographic requirements specific to each award
